Building a Standing Desk

{{template "side" .}}

After reading about standing desks and getting back pain from the cheap chairs at work, I decided to ditch sitting and embrace vertical computing at home. Being a hacker/maker I decided to build one myself, with much assistance from my brother who graciously supplied the workspace and the wood.

Now that I have been using a standing desk for a few years, I've noticed a few things. First, whenever I feel like I want to get a drink or go check out something in the other room, I just go. Before I felt like I would wait for a good opportunity to walk around, but since I'm standing already I just go for it. Next, I move very often while standing. I sway back and forth, kick back a leg now and then, and lean back. It's very natural and I do it without thinking. My final observation is it makes me feel lazy to watch TV while sitting. My PC is now healthier for me to use than the TV!
