Arduino Alarm System

  • 1 Arduino
  • 1 ethernet shield
  • 1 piezo buzzer
  • 1 magnetic switch
  • 2 resistors (1k and 10k)
  • 2 long strands of 22 gauge wire

The system is not connected to a computer. Instead it uses the Ethernet Shield to directly send emails. This means it is a low power solution. Battery backup would be pretty cheap to implement as a result. I get emails and texts when:

  • The door opens
  • The door is held open for more than 10 seconds
  • The Arduino reboots

These three conditions are enough to indicate when power goes out, or when the sensor falls off the door to help reduce false positives.
